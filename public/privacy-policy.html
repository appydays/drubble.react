ScramAir Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how DPDigital ("we," "us," or "our"), the individual developer of ScramAir, collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of the ScramAir game (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner, in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Who We Are

ScramAir is developed and operated by DPDigital, an individual self-employed IT consultant based in the United Kingdom.

Contact Email for Privacy Matters: support@scramair.cymru

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data when you use the App:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your:
    • Email Address: Used for login, account management, and, if explicitly opted-in, marketing communications.
    • Nickname: A unique identifier within the game.
    • Full Name (Optional): If you use third-party authentication (e.g., Google Sign-On), your full name as provided by that service may be collected.
    • Third-Party Authentication IDs: Unique identifiers provided by third-party login providers (e.g., Google ID, Apple ID, Facebook ID, if implemented).
  • Communication Preferences:
    • Receive Newsletter: By default, set to false. We will only send you newsletters if you explicitly opt-in through your account settings.
    • Receive Prompts/Reminders: By default, set to false. We will only send you game-related prompts or reminders if you explicitly opt-in through your account settings.
  • Log Data: When you access or use the App, we automatically collect certain information that your device sends. This may include:
    • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address.
    • Browser type and version.
    • Operating system.
    • The times and dates of your access.
    • Other diagnostic data related to the App's performance and usage.
  • Cookies: We use strictly necessary cookies for the purpose of managing your login session within the App. These cookies are essential for the App to function correctly and do not store any personal information beyond what is required for authentication.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: This includes managing your account, enabling login, and ensuring the App's functionality. (Legal Basis: Performance of a contract)
  • To Improve the App: We use aggregated and anonymized log data to understand how the App is used, troubleshoot issues, and enhance user experience. (Legal Basis: Legitimate interests in improving our services)
  • For Security and Fraud Prevention: To protect the integrity and security of the App and our users, and to detect and prevent fraudulent activity. (Legal Basis: Legitimate interests in protecting our business and users, compliance with legal obligations)
  • To Communicate with You:
    • Account-related communications: Sending essential service messages, such as password reset instructions. (Legal Basis: Performance of a contract, legitimate interests in managing user accounts)
    • Marketing & Prompts (Opt-in only): If you have explicitly opted in, we will send you newsletters or game-related prompts/reminders via email. You can withdraw your consent at any time through your account settings. (Legal Basis: Consent)
  • For Future Personalization and Advertising: We currently do not use your data for personalization or advertising within the App. However, should we introduce such features in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and obtain your explicit consent or identify a clear legal basis before processing your data for these purposes. (Legal Basis: To be determined upon implementation, likely Consent or Legitimate Interests with appropriate safeguards).

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties for their marketing or commercial purposes. We only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our App (e.g., hosting providers, email service providers for account-related emails). These third parties have access to your personal data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
  • Business Transfers: If DPDigital is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal data may be transferred. We will provide notice before your personal data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

When your account is deleted, or you request deletion of your data, we will securely delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required for legal or regulatory reasons.

6. Your Data Protection Rights (UK GDPR)

As a data subject under UK GDPR, you have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • The Right to Be Informed: To be informed about how your personal data is collected and used (this Privacy Policy serves this purpose).
  • The Right of Access: To request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • The Right to Rectification: To request that inaccurate or incomplete personal data be corrected.
  • The Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten"): To request the deletion of your personal data where there is no compelling reason for its continued processing. Users can initiate account and data deletion directly within the App's settings, or request it by contacting support@scramair.cymru.
  • The Right to Restrict Processing: To request the suspension of the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • The Right to Data Portability: To request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • The Right to Object: To object to the processing of your personal data, particularly where we are relying on legitimate interests as the legal basis for processing.
  • Rights in Relation to Automated Decision-Making and Profiling: We do not currently use automated decision-making or profiling that would have a significant impact on you.
  • The Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process your data, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@scramair.cymru. We will respond to your request within one month.

7. International Data Transfers

As we are based in the UK and your app can be used globally, your data may be processed outside the UK. When transferring data outside the UK, we will ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions) to protect your personal data in accordance with UK data protection laws.

8. Children's Privacy

While ScramAir has no specific age restrictions, it is primarily targeted at users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you learn that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@scramair.cymru. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We will also inform you via email or a prominent notice within the App prior to the change becoming effective. Your continued use of the App after the effective date of the revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the terms.
